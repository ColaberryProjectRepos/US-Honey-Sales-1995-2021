Creating Measures and Columns 12

  1. For Calculated Columns. Select Us Honey Data Table and create a new column. In the formula, the bar provides the DAX formula press Enter or select the checkmark icon, and Create Columns. 
  2. Creating our measures table. You do not have to do this step! But if you want to organize your data better this is how you do it. Go to the "Home" tab and click "Enter Data" and just rename the blank table to Measures Table" click OK, Then start adding measures.
Calculated Columns:
  1. ..Sales = 'US Honey Data'[production] - 'US Honey Data'[stocks]
  2. ..Value of Stocks = 'US Honey Data'[value_of_production] -'US Honey Data'[..Value of Sales]
  3. ..Value of Sales = 'US Honey Data'[..Sales] * 'US Honey Data'[selling_price]
Calculated Measures:
  1. Total Sales = Sum('US Honey Data'[..Value of Sales])
  2. Total Production = Sum('US Honey Data'[value_of_production])
  3. Sales LY = CALCULATE(Sum('US_honey_production_sales_1995_2021'[..Value_of_Sales]), DATEADD('Calender'[Date], -1, YEAR))
  4. SalesYOY = CALCULATE(Sum('US_honey_production_sales_1995_2021'[..Value_of_Sales])- 'Measure_Table'[..Sales LY])
  5. Sales YOY% = DIVIDE(SUM('US_honey_production_sales_1995_2021'[..Value_of_Sales]) - 'Measure_Table'[..Sales LY], 'Measure_Table'[..Sales LY])

  6. Arrow Sales = SWITCH(TRUE(),('Measure_Table'[SalesYOY])>0,UNICHAR(9650), ('Measure_Table'[SalesYOY])<0,UNICHAR(9660)," ")

  7. Color Arrow Sales = IF ('Measures_Table'[Arrow Sales] = UNICHAR(9660), "Red", "Green")
  8. Profit = 'Measure_Table'[..Total Production Cost] - 'Measure_Table'[..Total Sales]
  9. GPM% = DIVIDE('Measures_Table'[Profit], 'Measures_Table'[Total Sales],0)
  10. GPM_LY% = CALCULATE('Measure_Table'[..GPM%], DATEADD('Calender'[Date], -1, YEAR ))
  11. GPM_YOY% = DIVIDE('Measures_Table'[GPM%] - 'Measures_Table'[GPM_LY%], 'Measures_Table'[GPM_LY%])

  12. Arrow GPM YoY % =

    SWITCH(TRUE(),('Measure_Table'[GPM_YOY%])>0,UNICHAR(9650), ('Measure_Table'[GPM_YOY%])<0,UNICHAR(9660)," ")

  13. Color Arrow GPM YOY% = IF ('Measures_Table'[GPM_YOY%] = UNICHAR(9660), "Red", "Green")
